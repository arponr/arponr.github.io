Work and Not Work by Arpon Raksit

Night poem II

my memories don’t dry up,
    i am the bathing bird.

how did you, like that plane, vanish
    into the cloud?

in my eyes, the remnant sky
    becomes a few blue flowers.

under my hovering hands, i feel fabric,
    checkered, undulating.

i turn off my light,
    i vanish into the natural night.
