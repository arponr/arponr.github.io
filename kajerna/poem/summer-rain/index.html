Work and Not Work by Arpon Raksit

summer rain

the summer rain soothes me:
you are alone now, it tells me,
you will always be alone.

i walk slowly, waiting
at crosswalks, letting
others ahead, soaking.

the summer rain is a lie
i tell myself, i tell myself.

a fallen tree blocks a road.

buds bloom variably up a stem.

somebody is redirecting traffic.
from where does her music play?
