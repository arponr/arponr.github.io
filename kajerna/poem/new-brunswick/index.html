Work and Not Work by Arpon Raksit

New Brunswick

The roundabout is empty,
And you go left, I right.
That is, you enter where others do not.
That is because of all that you have read
And all that your mother and father read.
I’m doing my best to read closely what they wrote
Despite the slightness of my senses, of my heart-thoughts…
With your sharp face and mind, with ease
You have entered there, you are centered there.
And you are right, I am the one-dimensional mast,
The two-dimensional sail, the once-forgotten wind,
I am all that is left moving in your wake.
