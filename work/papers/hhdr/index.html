Work and Not Work by Arpon Raksit

Hochschild homology and the derived de Rham complex revisited

Submitted

Develops notions of “homotopy-coherent cochain complex” and “filtered circle action”, and uses these to characterize the derived de Rham complex and HKR-filtered Hochschild homology by universal properties, as well as reconstruct the filtrations on negative and periodic cyclic homology relating these invariants to derived de Rham cohomology.
